Career Paths After Matric

  • Software Developer: Build and maintain software applications. Requires Maths and Computer Applications Technology (CAT).
  • Teacher: Educate learners at various levels. Requires English and Life Orientation.
  • Nurse: Provide healthcare support. Requires Life Sciences and Maths or Maths Literacy.
  • Electrician: Install and maintain electrical systems. Requires Technical subjects and Physical Sciences.
  • Entrepreneur: Start and manage businesses. Requires Business Studies and Economics.
  • Graphic Designer: Create visual content for media. Requires Art and Design skills.
  • Mechanical Engineer: Design mechanical devices and systems. Requires Maths and Physical Sciences.
  • Environmental Scientist: Study environment and sustainability. Requires Life Sciences and Geography.
